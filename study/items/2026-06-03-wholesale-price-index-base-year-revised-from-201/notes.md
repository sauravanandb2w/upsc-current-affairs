## Summary / story

<p>India is revising the WPI base year from 2011–12 to 2022–23 and gradually transitioning toward Producer Price Index (PPI) to modernize inflation measurement and align with global standards.</p>

## Facts

<ul><li><p>WPI basket expanded:</p><ul><li>697 → 957 items</li></ul></li><li><p>Weight structure broadly unchanged:</p><ul><li>Manufactured products still have highest weight (~63%).</li></ul></li><li><p>Government will experimentally introduce:</p><ul><li>Producer Price Index (PPI)</li></ul></li><li><p>After around 5 years, WPI may be replaced by PPI.</p></li><li><p>Services PPI will also be introduced for:</p><ul><li>banking,</li><li>telecom,</li><li>insurance,</li><li>railways etc.</li></ul></li><li><p>WPI is released monthly.</p></li><li><p>Reserve Bank of India mainly uses CPI for inflation targeting.</p></li></ul>
